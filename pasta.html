Pasta Recipe

"Life is a combination of magic and pasta."

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Garlic cloves
  • Onion (optional)
  • Tomatoes or tomato sauce
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Fresh basil or dried basil
  • Parmesan cheese or any cheese of your choice
  • Fresh or dried oregano (optional)
  • Red pepper flakes (optional, for a bit of spice)
?

Steps

  1. Boil Water: Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add a generous pinch of salt to the water (this adds flavor to the pasta).
  2. Cook Pasta: Add the pasta to the boiling water. Cook according to the package instructions until al dente (usually around 8-12 minutes). Stir occasionally to prevent sticking.
  3. Prepare Sauce: While the pasta is cooking, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add minced garlic and chopped onions (if using) and sauté until fragrant and softened.
  4. Add Tomatoes: Add diced tomatoes or tomato sauce to the skillet. Stir to combine and let it simmer for about 10-15 minutes. Season with salt, black pepper, basil, oregano, and red pepper flakes (if using).
  5. Drain Pasta: Once the pasta is cooked, reserve a cup of pasta water, then drain the rest. Add the pasta to the skillet with the sauce.
  6. Combine: Toss the pasta in the sauce until well coated. If the sauce is too thick, add a bit of the reserved pasta water to achieve the desired consistency.
  7. Serve: Plate the pasta, and sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil.
  8. Enjoy: Your delicious pasta dish is ready to be enjoyed. Bon appétit!
