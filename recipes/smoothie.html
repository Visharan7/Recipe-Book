Blueberry Smoothie Recipe

“Sip your way to health with a refreshing smoothie.”

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons Oats
  • 1 cup Fresh Spinach
  • 1 cup Blueberries-frozen
  • 1/3 cup Plain Greek Yogurt
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 0.12 teaspoon Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Add oats and fresh spinach to the blender.
  2. Add the Frozen blueberries
  3. Add rest of the ingredients and blend it up until smooth.
  4. Serve immediately with some fruit & nuts topping you love.
