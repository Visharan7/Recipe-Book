Creamy Chicken Alfredo Recipe

“A Creamy and comforting classic that's perfect for any.”

Ingredients

  • 2 Chicken Breasts
  • Pasta
  • 3 cloves garlic-minced
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Salt, Pepper and parsley
  • 1 cup milk

Steps

  1. Cook the Pasta: Cook pasta in boiling salted water, drain, and set aside.
  2. Cook the Chicken: Season chicken with salt and pepper. Cook in olive oil for 6-7 minutes per side, then slice.
  3. Make the Sauce: Sauté garlic, add heavy cream and milk, simmer. Stir in Parmesan and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Combine: Toss pasta with sauce, add chicken slices, and mix well.
  5. Serve: Garnish with parsley and serve!
