Pancake Recipe

“There is hardship in everything except eating pancakes.”

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup buttermilk
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter

Steps

  1. Whisk dry ingredients.
  2. Combine wet ingredients in a separate bowl.
  3. Mix wet into dry; don’t overmix.
  4. Heat a greased pan over medium.
  5. Pour batter & cook till bubbles form. Flip & finish.
  6. Serve with toppings you love.
